RETURN TO SENDER

In Fall 2016, I was the Art Director for the UCLA Senior Thesis film "Return to Sender". I assembled and coordinated a team of 5 artists to paint, dress, and decorate a set. In less than 2 days, we rented dozens of props from Warner Brothers, painted 3 rooms, and dressed an empty apartment from scratch. I was in charge of maintaining a visual look on screen and keeping props continuous throughout shots.
Costume Designer: Ally Kolsky; Hair and Makeup: Mariam Yaralyn; Set Dressers: Christopher Hayden, Faraz Alam; Art PAs: Rocky Avalos, Terrence Jerard Garcia

DOOR AT THE END OF THE WORLD

Recently, I wrote, produced, and directed a film on the Arri-S 16mm. The film is primarily set in a meadow that overlooks the Pacific Ocean. With a team of costume designers and artists, we built a surreal free-standing door, an elevator dial, and designed a Russian-Mongolian inspired costume.
Art Director: Vincent Blake; Costume Designer: Jenny Chen; Assistant Set Construction: Terrence Jerard Garcia
